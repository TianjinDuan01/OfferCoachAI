What's inside

Five features that work together

Not just a question bank. A complete coaching loop from JD to offer.

CompanyDNA
Surfaces real interview patterns from Glassdoor, Blind, and LinkedIn for your target company. Shows a confidence tier so you know exactly what data is driving the questions.
ResumeMatch
Parses your resume and maps your real experiences to each question. Suggests a personalized STAR framework before you answer — based on your actual background, not generic examples.
4-Dimension Scoring
Every answer is scored across Logic, Specificity, Role Relevance, and Clarity in real time — not after the session. You see exactly where you lost points and what to fix.
STAR Structure Coach
Detects which components of your answer are missing or weak. Prompts you to complete the Situation, Task, Action, and Result in real time — building the habit, not just flagging the gap.
Radar Growth Tracking
Saves scores across sessions and visualizes your skill growth on a radar chart. Progress becomes tangible — the key retention mechanic that keeps users coming back session after session.
Coaching, not practice
Every other tool gives you a question and waits for the next one. OfferCoach AI evaluates, identifies the gap, and tells you exactly what to say differently. That's the difference.

How OfferCoach AI Works
1
Input Layer
Company name triggers CompanyDNA detection. JD text is parsed by the LLM to extract role requirements, skills, and likely interview themes. Resume is parsed to extract experiences and achievements.
2
Question Generation
The system combines company interview patterns (T1/T2/T3), JD requirements, and resume gaps to generate tailored questions — not generic question-bank prompts.
3
ResumeMatch Scaffold
User experiences are mapped to each interview question. Before answering, the system suggests a personalized STAR framework based on the user's real background.
4
Answer Evaluation
The answer is evaluated across Logic, Specificity, Role Relevance, and Clarity. The AI returns structured JSON scores and the dashboard updates immediately.
5
Growth Tracking
Scores are saved across sessions. The radar chart visualizes interview skill growth over time — making improvement tangible and keeping users coming back.
